The boat sequence took nineteen months to shoot. three different leaf boats were constructed using natural elements such as leaves, sticks and branches. All the three boats required approximately 245,450 leaves to coat them

To create the golden glow for The Moon King a reflective golden material: Mylar was stuck inside the beast’s body - a material found in party balloons which had to be purchased from every shop in and around Portland.

At 1 hour and 41 minutes long, this is the longest stop-motion film to date, beating out Coraline (2009) by a minute, which was also from Laika Studios.

The Skeleton Demon (based on the Gashadokuro of Japanese folklore) is the largest stop-motion puppet to be built, standing at eighteen feet tall.
